Operating Guidelines

Shared standards for decision-making and collaboration

Faith-centered conduct
  • Align decisions with Islamic values
  • Uphold integrity, fairness, accountability
Clear, no-drama collaboration
  • Communicate openly and directly
  • No gossip or passive-aggressive behavior
  • Address issues calmly and constructively
Money as a tool, not a goal
  • Use wealth to enable purpose
  • Avoid status-driven decisions
Unity over ego
  • Prioritize team success over personal pride
  • Let go of the need for credit
Loyalty and support
  • Support each other, especially under pressure
  • Build trust through consistency
